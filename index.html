The Shop


Café Béni is a nice place where you can hang out or chill with friends and family. It gives a warm and cozy feeling and serves good and tasty food and drinks. And how they treat their customers and the experience gives in their coffee shop. A remarkable experience will keep people coming back, spending more time. All we can say is that your money is well spent at Café Béni.

Café Béni has already established its first branch in Mabalacat City and the other one is in Magalang. The name "Café Béni" originates from the French word for "blessed." The owners chose "béni" with the intention of evoking a sense of blessing forall who visit the cafe. They also provide a wide range of offerings, including main courses, breakfast options, appetizers,and both hot and cold beverages. Particularly noteworthy is the emphasis on what Café Béni represents beyond its offerings - encompassing the staff, service quality, facilities, and overall ambiance. The aim is to extend the Béni experience to provide employment opportunities and a welcoming atmosphere to a broader audience.

Exploring Café Béni's Products


Café Béni offers a diverse menu that includes both hot and cold beverages, main courses, breakfast options, desserts, and appetizers. Their popular beverages include the Caramel Macchiato, known for its rich espresso and creamy caramel blend, and the Butterscotch drink, which features a smooth, sweet flavor. In terms of food, the Bistek Negro, a savory dish that combines marinated beef with a unique black sauce, is a standout favorite among customers. Café Béni ensures that all their ingredients are of the highest quality and purity, reflecting their commitment to excellence in every item they serve.
